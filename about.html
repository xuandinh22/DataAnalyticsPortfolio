About Me

I'm Xuan Dinh, and I also go by Ophelia, a data analyst who approaches complex datasets with the methodical precision of a detective and the creative insight of a storyteller. My passion lies in transforming raw information into strategic intelligence that drives meaningful business outcomes. I view data analysis as the critical bridge between complexity and clarity. With my foundation in Economics and Statistics, I excel at uncovering hidden patterns, identifying actionable trends, and translating quantitative insights into strategic recommendations that create measurable impact. My long-term goal is to advance into data analytics consulting, where I can partner with organizations to solve their most challenging analytical problems.

Strengths

One of my greatest strengths is adaptability. I was an international student when I first came to the United States, leaving behind my family and everything familiar. Those early days were challenging and eye-opening, teaching me to thrive in ambiguous situations and approach unfamiliar challenges with curiosity rather than hesitation. This adaptability translates directly into my analytical work, where I navigate complex datasets and find innovative solutions to non-standard problems. I also see myself as both a dreamer and a doer. Dreaming fuels creativity, helping me explore problems from multiple perspectives and generate innovative ideas. Doing keeps me grounded, ensuring I transform ideas into tangible solutions that deliver measurable results.

Education & Skills

- Education: B.A. in Economics (Quantitative Analysis focus), Minor in Statistics, San Diego State University

- Core Skills: SQL, Excel (Advanced), Tableau, Statistical Analysis, Data Visualization

- Certifications: Tableau Business Intelligence Analyst, Excel Skills for Business, Google Data Analytics, SQL (LearnSQL.com)

- Specializations: Trend identification, dashboard development, translating complex findings into actionable business recommendations.
